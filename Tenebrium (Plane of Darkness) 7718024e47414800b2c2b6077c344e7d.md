# Summary

The Plane of Darkness is often Depicted in the southern side of the planar maps. When the worlds are orbiting through the plane of Dark, they have their night and moons, this period usually lasts about 12 hours while the other 12 are spent in the Plane of Light. On the edges of the planes, the differences between line and dark are diluted and called dawn or twilight.

The Plane of Darkness is knownor called as different names, Tenebrium, Nightfall, Plane of Darkness.

# Geography

[Tenebrium (Plane of Darkness)](Tenebrium%20(Plane%20of%20Darkness)%207718024e47414800b2c2b6077c344e7d.md) is a massive Flat Crescent shaped land, almost in the shape of a crescent moon with the wide part at the furthest from the [Prime Material Planes](Prime%20Material%20Planes%2020a880ed277044b892497e01fd27933b.md). The edges, sometimes known as the shores of darkness of the plane lead into nothingness, this is often called the Abyss, the dark empty space, any creature that steps into the abyss is lost forever. Mythis of the [Fiend](Fiend%205049cfc5296c4178bea52b4bbcb5a689.md)s will often mention the monsters that inhabit the abyss that snatch any creature that dares go close to the shores.

While being completely flat, [Tenebrium (Plane of Darkness)](Tenebrium%20(Plane%20of%20Darkness)%207718024e47414800b2c2b6077c344e7d.md) still has a functional gravity similar to the rest of the planes.

### Abbadon or ‘The Underworld’

Abbadon is the underground and inner of Tenerium, under the dark desolate crust, lies, a whole ecosystem that many mortals would not dare see.

The deeper you go into Abbadon, the ‘heavier’ gravity feels. This [**Hypergravity**](https://www.notion.so/Hypergravity-10575a22781a805eb3a6d50e1a47dc9b?pvs=21) makes travelling, and especially flying very difficult.

# Inhabitants

### Population

The Plane of Darkness is the home plane of the Fiends and is believed to be the [Fiend](Fiend%205049cfc5296c4178bea52b4bbcb5a689.md)’s origin. The people of [Tenebrium (Plane of Darkness)](Tenebrium%20(Plane%20of%20Darkness)%207718024e47414800b2c2b6077c344e7d.md) have an extreme variety of skills, inclinations and also nature. One can find the most cunning and resourceful Devils or meet the most feral of Demons. Often Darkness is associated with evil but the reality is that most people of [Tenebrium (Plane of Darkness)](Tenebrium%20(Plane%20of%20Darkness)%207718024e47414800b2c2b6077c344e7d.md) can still be good or evil.

### The Pentalords

[Tenebrium (Plane of Darkness)](Tenebrium%20(Plane%20of%20Darkness)%207718024e47414800b2c2b6077c344e7d.md) has a 5 way split both as geographic and political, The Pentalords also know as the Lords of Darkness or Archfiends, are the titles used for those that rule [Tenebrium (Plane of Darkness)](Tenebrium%20(Plane%20of%20Darkness)%207718024e47414800b2c2b6077c344e7d.md). As long as history is recorded there were always 5 of them and the shift in power with [Tenebrium (Plane of Darkness)](Tenebrium%20(Plane%20of%20Darkness)%207718024e47414800b2c2b6077c344e7d.md) is quite static.

Each of the Pentalords have their own Dukes or Duchesses that rule cities or area within their realms. They also have their own separate armies, laws and dogmas or beliefs.
